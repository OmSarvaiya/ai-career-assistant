Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +
Our free trial gives you 10 minutes of unlimited access to all AI interview assistance features. No credit card required, and you can use it on any supported video platform including Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet.
Which video platforms are supported? +
We support all major video conferencing platforms including Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Cisco Webex, Skype, GoToMeeting, and many others. Our Chrome extension works seamlessly across all these platforms.
Is my interview data secure and private? +
Yes, absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect all your data. Interview conversations are processed securely and are never stored or shared. We're fully GDPR and CCPA compliant.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team for a full refund within 30 days of your purchase.
How does the AI generate responses? +
Our AI analyzes interview questions in real-time and generates personalized responses based on your background, the job requirements, and best practices for interview success. The responses are tailored to sound natural and professional.
